Future project ideas

Python AI for identifying notes in a song's melody

  • train AI to identify pitches and lengths of pitches
  • use pitch/note data to generate usable midi files for DAW software

  • Starting point: train AI to identify one note/pitch (e.g. A#, Bb, etc.)
    • dataset should include sound samples from the same pitch being played by a variety of different sources (traditional instruments, midi digital instruments, different voices, instruments that have effects applied [e.g. delay, reverb, chorus, phasers])
    • datasets should contain identical pitches that are being played for different durations, from extremely short 64th notes to notes that last for multiple bars/measures

Python AI map generator for other planets

  • "What would it look like if humans built cities on the moon? On Mars?"
  • I want an AI that will be trained to create hypothetical human-civilization-like maps on other planets, moons, solid heavenly bodies.

  • Starting point: train AI to generate fictional roadmaps based on Earth maps.
  • Then train AI to adjust maps based on terrain, elevation
